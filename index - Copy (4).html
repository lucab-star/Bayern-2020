TROPHIES

All the trophies bayern won that season

Bundesliga

The first trophy bayern won this season was on june 16th on a 1-0 win against Werder Bremen, the Bundesliga.
This is their regular season trophy with only German teams that they won. They won the league by 13 points over second place Dortmund their rivals.

DFB-POKAL

The second trophy they won this season was on the 4th of July with a 4-2 win against Bayer Leverkusen.
This trophy is the domestic cup with only german teams.

UEFA Champions League

The third trophy that seaon was won on the 23rd of August with a 1-0 win against PSG.
The UCL is an annual tournament where all the best teams from europe play eachother and is the biggest cluv trophy in the world.

UEFA Super Cup

The fourth trophy won by Bayern in 2020 was on the 24th of September against Sevilla from Spain in the UEFA super cup.
The UEFA super cup is when the winner of the Europa league (the second tier of the europe cup) goes against the winner of the UCL in a one match tournament.
Bayern won this game and the trophy with a 2-1 win in extra time.

DFL-Supercup

Bayern won their 5th trophy of the season on the 30th of September against their rivals dortmund in a 3-2 thriller.
The DFL Super cup is when the league winners face the cup winner.
But in this year both the league and cup winner were Bayern, so the 2nd place in the league plays instead.

Club World Cup

On the 11th of Febuary 2021 the bi annual club world cup took place and Bayern won it.
its a tournament where the best team from each contenent compete against eachother.
Bayern beat Tigre in a 1-0 win to clinch their first Sextuple and the second Sextuple of all time.

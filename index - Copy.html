RECORDS

All record breaking things Bayern did that season

UCL win streak

Bayern were the first and only team to win every single match in a single UCL campaign.
there are 6 group stage matches and usually 7 knockout Matches.
but with covid that year it was cut down to 5, but still 11 match win streak nontheless.

Double treble

2020 Bayern were the second ever team to have won 2 trebles, one this year and one in the 2012/2013 season.
A treble is when a team wins their domestic league, domestic cup and the ucl
During 2020 only 7 clubs had a treble and Bayern became the second ever with two joining Barcalona

Most ucl goals

Bayern had a total of 43 goals in 11 games which is roughly 4 goals a game which is insane.
This had been the record of goals since they changed to the new format in 2003.

The most important fixtures that season

REGULAR SEASON

Bayern vs Dortmund (second match)

Even though bayern still had some games left in the season this game was basically the decider of who would win the bundesliga in 2020.
If bayern lost they still had a good chance to win it, but if dortmund lost it was basically over.
Bayern won the game 1-0 with a chip over the keeper in the 43rd minute.

Werder Bremen vs Bayern (second match)

This was the point were Bayern actually won the bundesliga, with two matches to spare this would put bayern in a poostition that dortmund couldnt reach.
The game was tougher than Bayern were expecting with Werder Bremen playing to their level the first 30ish minutes.
But Lewandowksi scored a 43rd minute half volley, and from then on bayern held on and won the bundesliga. Winning the game 1-0.

Bayern vs Psg: Champions league final
